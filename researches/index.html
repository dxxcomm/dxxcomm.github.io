Reasearch

Our group will focus on the following topics:

Communicating science:

Communication science is the study of how people share information and interact with each other using different methods like speaking, writing, or using technology.

Health risk:

Research in health risk explores the factors that could potentially cause harm to people's physical or mental well-being. This field encompasses a wide range of studies, including: Identification of Risk Factors, Epidemiological Studies, Assessment of Risk Levels, Development of Prevention Strategies, Long-Term Monitoring and Evaluation, Psychological and Behavioral Research, Technological and Environmental Impact Studies, and Global Health Perspectives.

Environmental risk:

Research in environmental risk involves studying and analyzing factors in the environment that could potentially cause harm to human health, ecosystems, or the planet as a whole. This field covers various aspects: Identification of Environmental Hazards, Assessment of Risk Levels, Exposure Analysis, Impact Studies, Development of Mitigation and Adaptation Strategies, Policy and Regulatory Research, Public Health Connections, and Climate Change and Global Issues.
